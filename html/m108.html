The soul that walked between delicate groves, now

and then formulating a phrase to be taken down by the young

secretary Thomas Hobbes, kept to a narrow path between

Bacons contempt was for hyperbole; not for the art of

speech. He was teaching the ranting players to speak

slowly, closely, subtly. He was proving that there is another

kind of poetry, the poetry of prose. He was bringing the

prose of the mind into being. And thus by increasing the

range of the poet, bu making it possible for him to express

more, he was making an end of anonymity.
